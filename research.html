Research Publication CV
My primary research interest is in the relationship between the mental and physical aspects of language. I am particularly interested in non-dualistic explanations. I pursue this interest using experimental methods and the mathematics of nonlinear dynamics.

However, I am excited by many topics in linguistics. I am also working on understanding noun phrase semantics from a cross-linguistic perspective with Veneeta Dayal, and investigating bilingual language processing using visual world eye-tracking with the Second Language Acquisition Lab at CUNY.

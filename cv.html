My primary research goal is to use language as a window through which to better understand the relationship between the mind and the physical world. Broadly, I am interested in the relationship of linguistic knowledge to its physical implementation.

Most recently, my research has investigated the phonetic effects of lexical and phonological contrast using the dynamical model of speech planning developed by Roon and Gafos (2016).
